#### Research Profile
- Research directions: My research focuses on <strong>computing-in-memory based on advanced memory technologies</strong>, with an emphasis on <strong>computing-in-memory chips based on emerging non-volatile devices</strong> and <strong>three-dimensional computing-in-memory architectures for large models</strong>. Keywords: Hf-based ferroelectric memory, computing-in-memory, large models.
